🎮 How to Play the Games

🎯 Standard Quiz Mode

Perfect for: Testing your music knowledge with friends!

💡 Recommended: Use the General Music Quiz for this mode! The decade-specific quizzes (50s, 60s, 70s, etc.) have all songs within a 10-year span, making the year-guessing extremely difficult unless you really want to test your expert music knowledge!
  1. Open one of the quiz game web pages (links below)
  2. Enter player names (up to 6 players)
  3. Click "Start Game"
  4. Use the app to scan the QR code
  5. Listen to the song and guess:
    • 🎵 Song Name
    • 🎤 Artist/Band
    • 📅 Release Year
  6. For each correct guess, click the corresponding button to award a point
  7. Click "Next Player" to continue
  8. Player with the most points wins!

🎲 Alternate Mode (Hitster-Style Timeline Game)

Perfect for: A fun timeline challenge!

💡 Works with all games! The decade-specific quizzes are great for Hitster mode - they make it more challenging since you need to guess the exact year within a narrow range!

What you need: 10 small pieces of paper per player

  1. Each player writes a random year between 1920-2025 on a piece of paper
  2. If are playing a specific decade start with the middle year to make it easier
  3. Place the paper in front of you
  4. Use the app to scan a song
  5. Guess if the song was released BEFORE or AFTER your written year
  6. If correct: Write the actual song year (shown on quiz page) on a new piece of paper and place it in the correct chronological position in your timeline
  7. If incorrect: Don't write anything - next player's turn
  8. Goal: First player to create a timeline of 10 correctly ordered cards wins!
💡 Strategy Tip: Start with a year like 1975 to give yourself flexibility for both earlier and later songs!

❓ Troubleshooting

App won't install: Make sure "Install Unknown Apps" is enabled in Settings
QR code won't scan: Ensure good lighting and hold phone steady
Song won't play: Check that you're logged into Spotify and have an active internet connection
App crashes: Close and reopen both Spotify and the quiz app
